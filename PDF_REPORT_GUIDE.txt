💰 WHAT GETS CALCULATED AUTOMATICALLY:
================================================================================

FINANCIAL CALCULATIONS:
──────────────────────
✓ Total Contributions = Sum of all member contributions
✓ Total Expenses = Sum of all expenses (all categories)
✓ Balance = Total Contributions - Total Expenses
✓ Category Percentages = (Category Total ÷ Total Expenses) × 100

DIESEL CALCULATIONS:
───────────────────
✓ Total Diesel Cost = Sum of all refill costs
✓ Total Diesel Quantity = Sum of all refill amounts (Liters)
✓ Average Price per Liter = Total Cost ÷ Total Quantity
✓ Fuel Efficiency = Total Distance ÷ Total Diesel Quantity (km/L)

DISTANCE CALCULATIONS:
─────────────────────
✓ Total Distance = Sum of distances between all GPS points
✓ Uses Haversine formula for accuracy
✓ Shown in kilometers with 2 decimal places

MEMBER TOTALS:
─────────────
✓ Each member's total = Sum of their contributions
✓ Grouped by member name
✓ Sorted alphabetically

EXPENSE TOTALS:
──────────────
✓ Each category total = Sum of expenses in that category
✓ Percentage calculated for each category
✓ All categories included
